About "Time in Motion"

Hex clock
Inspiration to use an SVG image

My first design idea, in which the minutes and seconds control the horizontal and vertican position, respectively, of the clock, and the hours control the font size, which cycles over a twelve-hour period:

This pair of images shows the same time but with the clock in slightly different positions, illustrating the change from discontinuous motion, where the position of the clock would update every second, to pseudo-continuous motion, where the position of the clock updates every 20 milliseconds and seems perfectly smooth.
